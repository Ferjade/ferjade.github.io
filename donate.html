Education

Education charities help the less fortunate students to finish their studies. Most of these charities donate to students regardless of age, educational track, or school. A lot of charities like this have set up their scholarship programs and schools where they can support students. Aside from scholarship programs, they also give financial aid, as well as the provision of learning materials.

Health

This is one of the most popular kinds of charities and there is a huge variety of organizations that fall under this category. Generally, charity groups like this provide help to people with physical disabilities or those who are sick. This covers providing money for medical research, paying hospital bills, and promoting health awareness.

Animal

Animal charities protect, support, and conserve animals and wildlife. This covers many types of charities such as pet and animal welfare organizations, and wildlife conservation organizations, among many others.
