Unicef Philippines

This is an agency of the United Nations that aims to serve children.

UNICEF Philippines implements different programs that cover nutrition, education, disaster risk reduction and emergencies, child protection, communication, HIV and AIDS prevention, and institutional development.

Green Earth Heritage

GreenEarth Heritage Foundation is a Christian humanitarian organization that addresses the holistic needs of underprivileged communities.

It covers a wide array of initiatives for social change such as sustainable livelihood in organic farming, educational initiatives, reforestation programs, and many more.

Haribon Foundation

This foundation supports the conservation of natural resources. It is committed to nature conservation through scientific excellence and community empowerment.

Pawssion Project

Pawssion project first started in Bacolod. After receiving tons of support from kind-hearted individuals, it opened a second shelter in Bulacan.

Now, there have rescued more than 1,000 animals that have suffered from injury, neglect, abuse, and abandonment.

Hound Haven Philippines

This is a non-profit organization that advocates for Contract Working Dogs or CWDs and Military Working Dogs or MWDs. They match these retired army dogs with new loving families.

Dream Big Pilipinas

Dream Big Pilipinas uses a multipronged approach to keep underprivileged kids off the streets. One of the major initiatives of this organization is to teach young girls and boys literacy.

It also helps to fight child labor as well as raise funds for scholarships. Dream Big Pilipinas also teaches kids football to instill a sense of discipline and purpose in them.
